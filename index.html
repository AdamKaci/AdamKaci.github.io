I led a school group project in which the goal we chose was to bring the most toys we could in 4 months to children in the Children's Hospital Los Angeles. We started with a $200 investment which we used to buy snacks. We then sold our goods and after 2 months of reselling, we made a profit of about $1,200. Our whole profit was used to buy toys which were given to children before the start of winter break.
